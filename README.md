# Linux-4-Beginners-Wiki

Linux-4-Beginners is my wiki project that intends to be the **definitive** wiki for those who want to get into Linux without giving a sh** about what the hell a Cronjob is or the difference between Intel and AMD. The average person should be able to pick up the front page of the Wiki, and by the 5th URL, be able to decide whether Linux is:
- Compatible for their use cases
- Compatible for their hardware
and also know why Linux is helpful.

## Who this is not targeted at

This is not targeted at anyone who already knows the names of more than 2 Linux distributions, or anyone who has heard of both the terms `archinstall` and `rpmfusion`. If you think that Linux, BSD, or even Unix is the saviour of free software, you probably know too much for this Wiki to mean anything, and _that is the point._ Your expertise will assist the creation of this.

## Personal Design Goals (can be discussed later)

**The user is the number 1 priority.**

Everything needs to be user friendly, and should not over-explain a concept.
Everything within the StartChain is intended to have minimal external URLs as to not confuse the user. 
Where possible, this wiki should be hardware agnostic as to not confuse the user.
If the user wants to learn more, we can guide them to the ArchWiki and Wikipedia.
