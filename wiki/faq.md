### How is Linux Free?

Volunteer work and donations. The Linux Foundation and KDE e.V. are both non-profits, and are legally required to not hold tons of money. If you wish to donate to a project, you can donate to [Linux Mint](https://www.linuxfoundation.org/about/donate), [KDE](https://kde.org/donate/), [LibreOffice](https://www.libreoffice.org/donate/), among thousands of other projects to keep the efforts going.
