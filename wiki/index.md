# Linux Beginner's Wiki

Linux is an alternative operating system to the standard Windows that is installed on the vast majority of computers sold worldwide. No matter your reason of ending up here, you are welcome to join the community. Linux is free of charge, and most software for it is also free.

## Where do I learn?
If you're a beginner, this is the Wiki for you. Linux is a whole world of philosophies and freedoms, but we'll dumb it down for you until you're familiar. Check out [[1-getting_started|Getting Started]] for an immediate jump into everything.
